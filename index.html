About Me

First of all, many thanks for coming to my site and giving me this opportunity to introduce myself. I Christian Sarazua, was born in Orange, NJ and brought-up in Linden, New Jersey. I attended Linden High School where my passion for technology blossomed. I was always fascinated by everything technology. In highschool, I took many electronics classes where they taught me how to create small robots and circuits. I ended up being the top student in many of those classes. After highschool, I was accepted into and attended Rutgers University in NJ. Originally, I wanted to major in Elecrical Engineering, but after taking one programming class, I immediately found myself passionate about programming. Since then I have been eager to learn anything and everything about development. Right after graduating, my family decided to move down to Florida. The timing was right, so I took a leap and tried my chances down in Florida. Shortly after moving, I was hired at Advent Software Inc., as a Client Support Representative. Fast forward one year and I was promoted to Client Support Specialist. Advent Software is an amazing company and I will be forever grateful for the opportunity that they gave me. A couple of months after that I found an opportunity at CSX for an entry level developer position. At CSX, I was assigned to various projects and used many technologies including Javascript, JQuery, HTML5, CSS, PL/SQL, ColdFusion, AJAX, and many more. My goal is to become a successful developer. My strengths are: Communication, Adaptability, Quick learning, Stress tolerance, Creativity, Self Motivated, Programming, Team Work, and Customer Service.
